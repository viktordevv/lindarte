Who We Are

Lindarte General Constructors is an insured licensed Latina-owned small business focused on making your home improvement ideas a reality while providing quality and fairly priced services that result in extraordinary transformations inside and outside your space.

Lindarte GC specializes in interior renovations to kitchens, basements, bathrooms, living rooms, and any room in your house that you want to see modified for the better. Our expertise also includes yard clean up and remodeling as well as home office renovations to bring that extra class to both your home and your workspace. In addition to this, we also provide our customers the option to get custom furniture that meets their every need and restyle accent walls in their home to fit their lifestyle and highlight any artwork they want to showcase. Contact us today for a quote and be one step closer to your dream space.
